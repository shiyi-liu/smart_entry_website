Feature Highlights

Community Manager

Allows the registration of additional person(s) who you can assign specific access right(s), from Disabled, View Only, or Full Access, to specific sections of the management portal, to help you manage your community.

Feature Highlights

Merchant List

Allows communities to create a phone directory of not only common phone numbers the residents may need to call, but also nearby business vendors who are interested in offering their services to the community's residents.

The vendor's business and phone number will then be prominently displayed in each resident's phone app. The HOA can then charge advertising fees to the vendors to have their business listed in the Merchant List, essentially turning our system into an income generating tool for the community.

Feature Highlights

Simple Installation

Allows users to use our service without a relay network connetion, Voice Over IP technology or a 4G connection.

Feature Highlights

Video Call

Unlimited live video calls for residents to see their visitors using their own smartphone before allowing access regardless where they are physically.
Voice only calls are also supported to any phone numbers within the US and Canada without any additional fees, you do not need any POTS line, VOIP, nor LTE services.

Feature Highlights

Full Solution

Full solution, supports both visitor entry management and access control to resident only areas.

Feature Highlights

Cashless Vending
